# inventoryPro

Welcome to **Inventory-Pro**, your easy-to-use platform for managing products, tracking stock, and keeping your business organized.
This quick guide will help you get set up and start using the system in just a few minutes.

# Accessing the Platform

Visit the web app at:

👉 **[InventoryPro](https://inventory-pro.onrender.com/)**

Inventory-Pro works on any device — computer, tablet, or phone. No installation required.

# Creating an Account / Logging In

1. Open the app.
2. Click **Sign Up** if you’re new.
3. Enter your details to create an account.
4. If you already have an account, click **Log In** instead.

After logging in, you’ll be taken directly to your **Dashboard**.

# Understanding the Dashboard

The dashboard gives you a quick overview of your inventory:

* 📦 **Total Products** — how many items you’re tracking
* ⚠ **Low Stock Items** — products running low
* 🔍 **Quick Search** — find products instantly
* 📝 **Recent Changes** — see what was recently updated

This page helps you understand your inventory health at a glance.

# Adding Your First Product

To begin tracking your inventory:

1. Go to **Products** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Add Product**.
3. Fill in the product details:

   * Name
   * Category
   * Quantity
   * Description (optional)
4. Click **Save**.

Your product will now appear in your inventory list.

# Updating Stock Levels

When stock changes (sales, restocks, etc.):

1. Open the **Products** page.
2. Select the product you want to update.
3. Change the quantity.
4. Save your update.

Your dashboard will reflect the new stock level instantly.

# Searching & Filtering Products

Inventory-Pro makes it easy to find items, even in large inventories.

### To search:

Use the search bar at the top of the Products page.

### To filter:

Use category filters to narrow down items (e.g., “Electronics,” “Clothing,” etc.).

# Low-Stock Alerts

Items with low quantities will appear in the **Low Stock** section of the dashboard
so you can reorder before running out.

This helps prevent stockouts and keeps your operations smooth.

# Editing or Removing Products

### Edit:

1. Open the product
2. Click **Edit**
3. Update details
4. Save changes

### Remove:

1. Choose the product
2. Click **Delete**
3. Confirm

(You can always add it back later.)

# Tips for New Users

* Keep product names consistent for easier searching
* Update inventory as soon as stock changes
* Use categories to organize large inventories
* Check the dashboard daily for low-stock items
